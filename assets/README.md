# pyEM Logo concept

The mark combines a broad, unshaded family-level distribution with a smaller shaded distribution and a centered MAP point estimate. The dashed vertical line anchors the estimate while keeping the nested structure legible at small sizes.

## Files

- `source/`: editable SVG masters with named groups and live text
- `svg/`: production SVGs, including outlined lockups and color variants
- `png/`: transparent PNG exports and square avatar files

## Typography

Inter, SIL Open Font License 1.1.

## Colors

- Charcoal: `#1F252B` | RGB 31, 37, 43 | CMYK 28, 14, 0, 83
- Deep Teal: `#0F5B63` | RGB 15, 91, 99 | CMYK 85, 8, 0, 61
- Soft Mint: `#7EC8BC` | RGB 126, 200, 188 | CMYK 37, 0, 6, 22

## Minimum size and clear space

Use the symbol at no less than 16 px high in digital contexts or 6 mm high in print. Maintain clear space equal to the diameter of the point-estimate dot on all sides.
